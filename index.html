About Us

A Networking Community
for Analytics Professionals

Hong Kong Analytics Community was founded in May 2015 by a small group of experienced analytics professionals in Hong Kong.
We want to create a community where analytics professionals from different backgrounds and different industrial can come
together to share ideas and inspire each other on how to use analytics in business.

Whether you are a student or a CEO, whether you are an academic or salesperson,
whether you work in financial services, telecommunications, retail, logistics, or any industry really,
as long as you have an interest in exploring how analytics might be able to help your business,
come along to one of our events.

You never know whom you might meet, or what you might learn from this community of curious,
engaged and inspiring analytics professionals.
